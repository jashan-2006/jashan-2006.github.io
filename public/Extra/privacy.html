Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 2025

1. Information We Collect

We may collect:

  • Personal information (name, email, age)
  • Game account information
  • Device and connection data
  • Usage statistics and gameplay data
  • Payment information for tournaments

2. How We Use Information

Your information helps us:

  • Provide and improve our services
  • Verify tournament eligibility
  • Personalize your experience
  • Communicate important updates
  • Ensure platform security

3. Data Sharing

We may share data with:

  • Tournament partners (only necessary information)
  • Service providers who assist our operations
  • Legal authorities when required by law

We never sell your personal data to third parties.

4. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures including encryption and access controls to protect your information.

5. Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13 without parental consent.

6. Your Rights

You may:

  • Access your personal data
  • Request corrections
  • Request deletion
  • Opt-out of marketing communications

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy periodically. We'll notify users of significant changes through our platform or email.

8. Contact Us

For privacy concerns, contact our Data Protection Officer at jaesports.humber@gmail.com.

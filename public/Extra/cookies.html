Cookie Policy

Last Updated: January 2025

1. What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit websites. They help sites remember information about your visit.

2. How We Use Cookies

We use cookies to:

  • Keep you signed in
  • Remember your preferences
  • Analyze site traffic
  • Improve user experience
  • Deliver targeted advertisements

3. Types of Cookies

Essential Cookies

Required for core functionality like login and security.

Performance Cookies

Collect anonymous data to improve our services.

Functionality Cookies

Remember your preferences and settings.

Advertising Cookies

Help deliver relevant ads and measure effectiveness.

4. Third-Party Cookies

We partner with services like Google Analytics that may set their own cookies. These are subject to their respective policies.

5. Managing Cookies

You can control cookies through your browser settings. However, disabling essential cookies may affect site functionality.

Our cookie consent banner allows you to customize preferences when you first visit our site.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy as our cookie usage evolves. Significant changes will be communicated to users.

7. Contact Us

For questions about our Cookie Policy, contact us at jaesports.humber@gmail.com.
